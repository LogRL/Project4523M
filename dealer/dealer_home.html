About Us

The Smart & Luxury Motor Company (Spares) Limited (SLMS) is a subsidiary of the Smart & Luxury Motor Company (SLMC), a public limited liability company. SLMC specializes in assembling and distributing motor cars, focusing primarily on the Chinese market. While SLMC operates in the motor car industry, SLMS is dedicated to providing spare parts to SLMC and selling spares to approximately 50,000 main dealers across various provinces and cities in the People's Republic of China (PRC).
